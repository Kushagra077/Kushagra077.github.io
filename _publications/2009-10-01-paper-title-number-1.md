This research work dives into an in-depth evaluation of the **YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once)** algorithm's efficiency in object detection, focusing specifically on **Barcode and QR code recognition**. Utilizing the real-time detection abilities of YOLOv8, we aimed to enhance its capability to swiftly and accurately identify objects.

Through extensive training and fine-tuning on **Kaggle datasets** tailored for Barcode and QR code detection, the study seeks to optimize YOLOv8's performance across various conditions and environments. The evaluation encompasses different model iterations: **Nano, Small, and Medium**, with careful attention to precision, recall, and F1 evaluation metrics.

The results highlight significant improvements in object detection accuracy with each subsequent model refinement. Specifically, we achieved an accuracy of **88.95%** for the Nano model, **97.10%** for the Small model, and **94.10%** for the Medium model, showcasing the impact of model scaling. These findings mark a notable advancement in **computer vision**, emphasizing YOLOv8's role as a milestone in object detection.
